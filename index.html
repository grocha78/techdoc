How to Make the Yummiest Quesadilla

    • Introduction

    We all love the taste of a freshly made, hot, cheesy gooey quesadilla. But have you ever tried to create one at home?

  • Keep reading if you want to know how to make your own yummy quesadilla in the comfort of your own home!
    • Ingredients

    Obviously, ingredients will vary depending on what kind of quesadilla you desire but we can at least start with the essentials.

  • Tortillas (preferably flour)
  • Cheese (queso quesadilla is the best option but whatever cheese you prefer)
  • Meat (beef or chicken)
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, cilantro, green onions)
    • Prepare Ingredients

    These are the best steps to follow so as to not take too much time.

  • Cut all vegetables into small edible pieces
  • Cut meat, as well, into small edible pieces
    • How to Cook

    Cooking and preparing your quesadilla is all about timing so pay attention on when to begin with the different ingredients.

  • Add some cooking oil to your pan and put the heat at just below medium
  • First add the mushrooms, bell peppers and onions (cilantro and green onions won't be added until the end)
  • Next, add the meat (keep the head at just under medium)
  • While you are doing this, on another flame, heat up the tortillas, just enough but not all the way, so that they be ready once the vegetables and meat are done
  • Put the tortilles on the side and put some cheese on one tortilla so it will be ready to go
  • Add the cilantro and green onions to the meat and vegetables and let them get cooked for a little bit
  • Once the meat and vegetables are done, place them on on the tortilla that had cheese
  • Place some more cheese on top of the meat and vegetables and using a spatula, place the quesadilla on the hot pan (let the tortilla soak up those yummy meat and vegetable juices!)
  • Toast on both sides so that the cheese will be evenly melted and voila! The hardest part is over!
    • How to Enjoy

    Now the best part, how to enjoy your homemade quesadilla!

  • Place the freshly made quesadilla on a cutting board and cut it up into four even pieces
  • Get your favorite condiments such as avocado and salsa
  • Cut that avocado into edible slices and pour some salsa on that quesadilla and enjoy!
