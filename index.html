    • Introduction

    We all love the taste of a freshly made, hot, cheesy gooey quesadilla. But have you ever tried to create one at home?

  • Keep reading if you want to know how to make your own yummy quesadilla in the comfort of your own home!
    • Ingredients

    Obviously, ingredients will vary depending on what kind of quesadilla you desire but we can at least start with the essentials.

  • Tortillas (preferably flour)
  • Cheese (queso quesadilla is the best option but whatever cheese you prefer)
  • Meat (beef or chicken)
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, onions, cilantro, green onions)
    • Prepare Ingredients

    These are the best steps to follow so as to not take too much time.

  • Cut all vegetables into small edible pieces
  • Cut meat, as well, into small edible pieces
